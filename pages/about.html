About

Erika Hance

I am an Indigenous-Canadian freelance illustrator and game artist based in Vancouver, British Columbia. I have a fondness for crafting cute and colourful visual identities, especially while designing characters.

Cute designs have more power than we give them credit for: they bring positivity to their viewers and they make brands more approachable and memorable. I am a firm believer that design should inspire strong emotions. After all, designing is a human-centric craft. Since spreading joy is my passion, I use my proficiency in Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI, Procreate, Pixaki and Adobe Suite Software to create cute designs that do just that. I am also familiar with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. I have worked on titles like Grooveyard (not yet released), and many individual projects.
