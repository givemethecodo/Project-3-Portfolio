I am an Indigenous-Canadian freelance illustrator and game artist based in Vancouver, British Columbia. I have a fondness for crafting cute and colourful visual identities, especially while designing characters.

Cute designs have more power than we give them credit for: they bring positivity to their viewers and they make brands more approachable and memorable. I am a firm believer that design should inspire strong emotions. After all, designing is a human-centric craft.
